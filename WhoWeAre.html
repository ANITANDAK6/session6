Who We Are

At Samuel, We are committed to becoming a world-class industrial products company and an exceptional place to work.

We offer a super experience to our customers by leveraging our unmatched supply chain, processing, engineering, design and manufacturing capabilities to deliver market-leading solutions and we offer that same great experience to our team members by providing countless growth and decision-making opportunities in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

70+ Locations

With facilities across Canada, the US, and Mexico we are well-positioned to serve customers anywhere in North America.

5,500+ Team Members

We offer our hardworking team members an array of benefits, on-the-job learning and opportunities for growth and development.

$4B+ Annual Revenue

We're committed to continuous improvement and are always looking for new ways to embrace innovation and grow our business.

Our Core Values

We have established a workplace that is differentiated by its inclusivity, opportunity and empowerment. These attributes are ingrained in our culture, employee programs and the five core values that drive the way we do business, every day.

Respect

Be open, inclusive and collaborative, with respect for diversity in all forms.

Agility

Empower people, acknowledge and learn from mistakes, course correct quickly.

Ownership

Be accountable to yourself and your team, deliver on commitments.

Courage

Think boldly, constructively challenge the status quo, strive for more.

Integrity

Be honest and always do the right thing.

Samuel Communities

Driven entirely by our employees, Samuel Communities is a company-wide corporate giving program that allows team members to submit causes close to their hearts for financial support from the business. To date, charities have included everything from animal shelters and food banks to toy drives and humanitarian support, at the local, national and international level. We look forward to continuing to support these organizations and many others in the years ahead.
