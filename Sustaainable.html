Since 1855, Samuel has understood that true success comes from focusing on the people and places that make our work possible - a principle that supports our vision for sustained growth. We operate in, helping our people grow and develop and continuing to be a truster partner to our customer and suppliers.

Our five core values - Integrity, Respect, Ability, Courage, and Ownership - are the foundation of everything we do. They guide our high ethical standards, help ensure a safe and productive environment impact, and support our commitment to giving back. This integrated approach is how we build a stronger, more responsibnle future and create meaningful impact across our businesses.

Environment

With over 70 locations across North America, Samuel requires significant energy to remain operational. We're committed to using the cleanest energy, where possible, to safeguard the environment and ensure longevity of our business.

Beyond energy, we have several waste reduction projects underway including recycling and reusing old hardware to minimize our footprint.
